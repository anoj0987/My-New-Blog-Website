Cheat's Turkish pizzas that are loaded with flavour

Here’s a quick and easy recipe for cheat's Turkish pizzas, also known as Lahmacun, a dish that’s loaded with flavor but requires minimal effort. This version uses store-bought flatbreads or tortillas to speed up the process while keeping the authentic flavors.


Ingredients:


1. 4 large store-bought flatbreads, tortillas, or thin pizza bases

2. 300g ground lamb or beef

3. 1 small onion, finely chopped

4. 2 cloves garlic, minced

5. 1 red bell pepper, finely chopped

6. 1 tomato, finely chopped

7. 2 tablespoons tomato paste

8. 1 teaspoon paprika

9. 1 teaspoon ground cumin

10. 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)

11. 1 teaspoon chili flakes (optional, for extra heat)

12. 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

13. Salt and pepper, to taste

14. Olive oil, for drizzling


For Serving:

Fresh parsley leaves

Lemon wedges

Sliced red onions

Yogurt or tahini (optional)


Instructions:


1. Prepare the Topping:

In a large bowl, mix together the ground lamb or beef, chopped onion, garlic, red bell pepper, tomato, tomato paste, paprika, cumin, cinnamon (if using), chili flakes, chopped parsley, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Mix until all the ingredients are well combined.


2. Assemble the Pizzas:

Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F). Lay the flatbreads or tortillas on a baking tray lined with parchment paper. Evenly spread a layer of the meat mixture over each flatbread, pressing it down slightly so that it adheres.


3. Bake:

Drizzle a little olive oil over the top of the pizzas. Bake in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes or until the meat is fully cooked, and the edges of the flatbreads are crispy.


Garnish and Serve:

Once baked, garnish with fresh parsley leaves, sliced red onions, and a squeeze of lemon juice. Serve with yogurt or tahini on the side for an added creamy element.


4. Tips:

You can use minced chicken or turkey for a lighter option. If you prefer a vegetarian version, substitute the meat with a mix of finely chopped mushrooms and lentils.


Enjoy these delicious, flavorful Turkish-style pizzas in under 30 minutes!
