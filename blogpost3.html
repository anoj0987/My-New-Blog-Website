World of Madras Chicken Curry, also known as Kari Kuzhambu. This dish is a staple in Tamil Nadu cuisine, known for its bold spices and deep flavors.


Ingredients


Chicken: 500g, cut into pieces. Onions: 2 , finely chopped. Tomatoes: 2 large, chopped. Ginger-Garlic Paste: 1 tbsp. Coconut Milk: 1 cup. Curry Leaves: A handful. Mustard Seeds: 1 tsp. Fennel Seeds: 1 tsp. Turmeric Powder: 1/2 tsp. Red Chili Powder: 2 tsp. Coriander Powder: 2 tsp. Garam Masala: 1 tsp. Salt: taste. Oil: 2 tbsp. Water:

needed.


Preparation Method

Spice Base: Heat oil in a deep pan. Add mustard seeds and fennel seeds, allowing them to splutter. Then, add curry leaves and saut√© for a few seconds. and cook until the raw smell disappears.


Tomatoes and Spices

Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft. Stir in turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook this mixture until the oil separates from the masala.


Cooking the Chicken

Add the chicken pieces and mix well, ensuring they are coated with the masala. Cook on medium heat for 5-7 minutes.


Simmering

Pour in coconut milk and a little water to adjust the consistency. Cover the pan and let it simmer on low heat until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.


Finishing Touch

Stir in garam masala and simmer for another 5 minutes. If the curry is too thick, adjust with more water.


Serving

Serve hot with steamed rice, dosa, or roti.
